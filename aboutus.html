About Philproperties International Corporation

PHILPROPERTIES INTERNATIONAL CORPORATION (PPIC) is a dynamic company that offers comprehensive service to cover all clientele real estate needs. Established in 2006, it is home to professional realtors, consultants and agents with varying specialities for the stress-free buying, selling and renting experience or any other real-estate needs on the part of its clientele. The company is relentless in its pursuit for excellence to ensure customer satisfaction with secure and sound real estate investments.

PPIC is currently specialized in the Mega-Manila area private properties and commercial developments. We have partnered up with all major real estate developers that include Ayala Land, Avida, SMDC, DMCI, Robinson's Land, Vista Land, Eton Properties, Duraville, Sta. Lucia Realty, Genato Management Corporation among many others.

With the wealth of experience of its founder, Manny Florendo and partners since 1996, PPIC has rapidly grown to be amongst the most reputable and reliable real estate management companies in the country.

MISSION

  • Customer satiscation
  • Develop teams with well-nourished culture based familial values galvanized by training to promote the efficiency and effectiveness.
  • The harnessing and sharing of opportunities for mutual benefit to forge a bright and vibrant lifetime career for everyone based on empathy and mutual respect.

VISION

"A one-stop robust, reliable and dynamic company that will ensure the satisfaction of its clientele’s real estate needs, the realization of their dreams and the solution to the challenges they face."
